Assignment 1
Introduction
Tech demo showing QuadTree, SceneGraph, Raycast Collision, Level of Detail. Frustum Culling and more.

Controls:
[3] Render all objects.
[4] Wireframe mode.
[Backspace] Spawn Monk.
[I] Spawn Crate.
[O] Spawn Buildings
[P] Spawn Walls.
[K] Toggle Hitboxes.
[L] Spawn Towers.
[Spacebar] Jump.
[Enter] Toggle QuadTree.
[Left Shift] Run.
[H] Toggle Hitscan.
[G] Reset.
[M] Bird-eye camera.
[Left Click] Shoot based on hitscan toggle.
[Right Click] Throw grenade.
